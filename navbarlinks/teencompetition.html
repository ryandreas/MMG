ABOUT THE MISS MIAMI GARDENS' TEEN SCHOLARSHIP COMPETITION.



Candidates in the Miss Miami Gardens' Teen Competition gain experience through participation in interviews,

talent performances, public speaking, promoting Social Impact Initiatives, and social media interaction.

Cash and in-kind educational scholarships are available to all young women who advance to Miss Florida's Teen State Competition.





PHASES OF COMPETITION:



  1. 1. Private Interview
  2. 2. On Stage Conversation
  3. 3. Health & Fitness
  4. 4. Performance Talent/HERStory
  5. 5. Evening Gown





MISS MIAMI GARDENS' TEEN COMPETITION SCORING



  1. 30% Private Interview
  2. 20% Performance Talent/HERStory
  3. 20% Health & Fitness
  4. 20% Evening Gown
  5. 10% On Stage Conversation





    6. SOCIAL IMPACT INITIATIVE



    1. Each candidate will be required to have a Social Impact Initiative which they will advocate during their year of service as Miss Miami Gardens. Each candidate should select a Social Impact Initiative in relation to a cause or issue that she has a strong personal commitment, involvement, or conviction.


    2. A candidate’s Social Impact Initiative allows her to explain to the judges and members of the community a subject matter of her choice, how she chooses to advocate for it during her year and the metrics she plans to use for success at the close of her year of service. In addition, it should explain how her advocacy will further the mission of the Miss Florida’s Outstanding TEEN & Miss America’s Outstanding TEEN Organizations.





      3. CANDIDATE ELIGIBILITY



      1. Miss Miami Gardens' Teen candidates must be between 13-18 years of age.


      2. Candidate must be a United States Citizen, who resides, works, or is successfully enrolled in and physically attending classes on a full-time basis at an accredited school, college, or technical school in the City of Miami Gardens, FL, or Opa-Locka, FL, reside, work, or attend school in one of the following approved address zip codes: 33013, 33014, 33054, 33055, 33056, 33169, 33175, 33179, 33269.





      ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS



      1. Candidates must be in reasonably good health to meet the job requirements.


      2. Participate in fundraising for the Miss America Foundation.


      3. Meet character criteria set forth by the Miss America Organization.


      4. Candidates must be able to meet the time commitment and job responsibilities as set forth by the Miss Miami Gardens Scholarship Program, Inc. should she win the title. (The titleholder will enter a contract with the organization)


      5. Candidates must not now, nor have ever been married, nor has had a marriage annulled, nor will get married during the year of service to the organization.





        6. APPLICATION



        1. First, you will need to register through the Miss America website. You will need to select the correct state (FLORIDA) and enter “Miss Miami Gardens” as the competition you would like to enter.


        2. Second, enter your information there to receive the application packet.
