Miss Miami Gardens Scholarship Program is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit that seeks to provide scholarship assistance to young women in Miami Gardens, FL. Our organization continues to provide women a dynamic platform to effectuate positive change in their communities through service and scholarship. Title holders are provided a year-round opportunity to earn academic assistance, grow their professional networks, learn empowering life skills, and be the change they want to see in the world. Join us as we continue to prepare great women for the world and prepare the world for great women.
